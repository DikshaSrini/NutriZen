Vegan Diet

A vegan diet is a plant-based eating approach that excludes all animal products, emphasizing fruits, vegetables, grains, and legumes. It is associated with various health benefits and promotes environmental sustainability by reducing the demand for animal agriculture.

Ketogenic Diet

The ketogenic diet is a low-carb, high-fat eating plan that aims to shift the body into a state of ketosis. This diet has been shown to have potential benefits for weight loss, blood sugar regulation, and improved mental clarity.

Raw Food Diet

The raw food diet is a dietary approach that emphasizes consuming unprocessed and uncooked foods, such as fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds. Advocates believe that this diet preserves the natural enzymes and nutrients in foods, promoting better digestion and overall health.
